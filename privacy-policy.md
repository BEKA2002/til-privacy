# Privacy Policy for TIL — Today I Learned

**Last updated: June 25, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how Bekbolat Samarkan ("I", "me", or "the developer") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the TIL — Today I Learned mobile application ("the App").

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information you provide
The App does not require account registration. No personal information such as name, email, or phone number is collected.

### Information stored locally on your device
The App stores the following data **only on your device** using AsyncStorage:
- Words you save to your personal dictionary
- Videos you have watched
- Your selected interface language (Russian, Kazakh, or Ukrainian)
- Your activity history (days active in the app)
- Cached word translations (to reduce API requests)

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

---

## 2. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

### Google Translate API
When you tap on a word to get a translation, the word is sent to the Google Translate API. This service is provided by Google LLC. Please refer to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for more information.

### Cloudflare R2
Video files are hosted on Cloudflare R2. When you watch a video, your device connects to Cloudflare's servers to stream the content. Please refer to [Cloudflare's Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/) for more information.

---

## 3. Video Content

All videos in the App are publicly available under Creative Commons licenses. Sources include:
- NASA (nasa.gov)
- VOA Learning English (learningenglish.voanews.com)
- The White House (whitehouse.gov)
- YouTube Creative Commons content

TIL does not use any private or copyrighted videos without permission.

---

## 4. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

---

## 5. Data Security

Since all user data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's security settings. I do not have access to any data stored on your device.

---

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the new Privacy Policy.
